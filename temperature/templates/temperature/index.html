Our Work

Raspberry Pi Interfacing


The project titled “REMOTE PLANT MONITORING SYSTEM” is deliberate to construct an monitoring mechanism which switches the water pump in between ON and OFF states on detecting the moisture level of the soil . The moisture is determined by calculating the conductivity or resistivity between these nails . If the moisture content of soil is low then the resistivity between the nails is high or the conductivity between them is low and as the moisture content increases conductivity increases and resistivity decreases . Apart from these we are using Ultra Sonic Sensors, GSM, Temperature Sensor, Motor Driver , humidity sensor.The advantage of using this system is to lessen human interference.
